Privacy Policy

Nyx processes limited data to deliver AI features, enforce safety, and maintain service reliability. This policy explains what information may be processed, how it is used, when it may be shared with providers, and what controls you have.

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Information Nyx May Process

Nyx may process your prompts, the surrounding selected channel context, optional model choices, and media you provide such as images, files, or URLs.

Depending on the command used, Nyx may also process OCR inputs, moderation requests, search queries, and generated outputs needed to respond in Discord.

How Data Is Used

Processed data is used to generate replies, perform requested tools (such as summarize, imagine, OCR, moderation, and search), and apply safety checks.

Nyx also uses this data for operational purposes such as rate limiting, abuse prevention, and maintaining service quality.

Storage and Retention

Conversation memory is temporary in-memory state. You can inspect, clear, disable, or enable it with /memory, and it resets on service restart.

Companion profile preferences configured via /profile are stored until you update or reset them.

Third-Party Processing

Nyx may send requests to ALabs AI SDK providers for chat, image generation, OCR, moderation, and related AI functionality.

When using /search, Nyx may retrieve public information from an allowlisted set of sources such as Wikipedia and selected news outlets.

User Controls

You can manage stored personalization and memory using /memory and /profile reset.

If you no longer want context used for responses, disable memory and avoid including prior-message references in requests.

Security and Sensitive Information

Do not share passwords, tokens, financial information, government identifiers, or other sensitive personal data in prompts or uploaded files.

While reasonable safeguards are applied, no internet-based service can guarantee absolute security.

Minors and Restricted Data

Nyx is not intended for unlawful collection of children’s data or regulated sensitive categories without appropriate authorization and safeguards.

Server administrators and users are responsible for using Nyx in compliance with local laws and platform rules.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated to reflect feature changes, provider changes, or legal and operational requirements. Material updates are reflected by the updated date above.

For project context and updates, see the open-source repository at github.com/axrxvm/nyx.
